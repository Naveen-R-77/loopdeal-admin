# LoopDeal Enterprise Rules & Guidelines

## 1. Store Vision
LoopDeal is the premier destination for high-performance network hardware, CCTV surveillance solutions, and enterprise computing in Bangalore. We prioritize security, reliability, and technical excellence.

## 2. Customer Service Principles
- Always greet customers with professionalism.
- Technical support for CP PLUS and HIKVISION products is available 24/7.
- Response time for critical network issues must be under 4 hours.

## 3. Shipping & Logistics Policies
- Standard shipping takes 3-5 business days across India.
- Logistics status "Delayed" must be actively monitored. If an order is delayed more than 48 hours, the customer should be contacted.
- Priority shipping is available for major enterprise infrastructure projects.

## 4. Product Warranty
Most CP PLUS, Dahua, and Hikvision products come with a 2-year manufacturer warranty. Refurbished Lenovo ThinkCentres carry a 1-year store warranty.

## 5. Refund Policy
- 15-day return policy for unused hardware in its original packaging.
- Technical installation fees are non-refundable.
